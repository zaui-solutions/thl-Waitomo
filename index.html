Zaui Pricing Proposal

At Zaui, we are highly flexible and willing to work with your budget to create a structure that works best for your business. We recognize that Waitomo operates across multiple lines of business, with different distribution channels, so our goal is to align pricing with both your operational model and your growth targets.

Option 1

Consumer-Funded (4% B2C Fee)

Fee: 4% charged only to your customers on the front end.

Condition: You must transact 30% or more through your website (direct channel).

  • If Under 30%: A fee is applied on OTA and backend bookings as well.
  • If Over 30%: OTA and backend bookings are free of charge.
  • Must choose Stripe as payment gateway.

Best for: Operators with a strong direct channel strategy who want costs shifted entirely to the end consumer.

Option 2

Variable-Only Model (0.99%)

Fee: 0.99% variable fee applied to all bookings (front-end, OTA, and backend).

Revenue Generator: Includes access to our fee-based revenue generator, enabling you to add a modest front-end service fee to offset system costs.

Best for: Operators wanting predictable alignment between system cost and business volume, with flexibility to recapture costs via service fees.

Best Option

Option 3

Hybrid Enterprise Model ($4,000/month + 0.6%)

Fee: $4,000 monthly flat fee + 0.6% variable fee applied to all bookings.

Revenue Generator: Includes access to our fee-based revenue generator, enabling you to add a modest front-end service fee to offset system costs.

Best for: Operators with high annual booking volume who want lower per-transaction costs, predictable base fees, and maximum long-term scalability.
